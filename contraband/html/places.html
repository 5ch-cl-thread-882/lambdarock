Places in Contraband

Atrobürg
Capital of the Kingdom of Atrocitas.
Bartertown
A trading town east in the Empire of Copia. West of the River Ovid.
Copia Diuturn
Capital of the Empire of Copia.
Dakau
Wild area north of Copia and Atrocitas that is dominated and ruled by fearsome Dogmen.
Lambda Rock
See Mont Renuo.
Mont Renuo
Also known as Lambda Rock. Easternmost town in the Empire of Copia and near the border to the Kingdom of Atrocitas.
Perseco
The battlefield where the elven forces rose up against the Empire and was beaten.
River Ovid
A long river that goes north-south in the Empire of Copia. Used for trading.
Venedig
Southern city in the Kingdom of Atrocitas.
